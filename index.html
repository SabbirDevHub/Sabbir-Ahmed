Research & Case Studies

IEEE Access (Q1 Journal) • Nov 7, 2025

Python Under the Microscope: A Comparative Energy Analysis of Execution Methods

Purpose: Explores how Python can be executed more efficiently while prioritizing sustainability for global computing demand.

Problem: Lack of data on energy use and carbon emissions of Python execution methods (interpreters, compilers, hybrids) impedes progress toward green software.

Solution: Conducted a comprehensive energy-performance analysis across CPython, PyPy, Cython, ctypes, and py_compile. Proposed GreenScore, a novel composite metric integrating energy, carbon emission, and execution time.

Contribution: GreenScore provides a unified benchmark to help developers identify energy-efficient environments and design responsible software.

Authors: Fatin Shadab, Md. Mahmudul Hasan, Mehbuba Khan, Naimur Navil, Adiba Irin, Sabbir Ahmed
Corresponding Author: Engr. Md. Motaharul Islam
